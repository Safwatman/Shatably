Building Design

Building design is a comprehensive process that involves conceptualizing, planning, and creating the layout, appearance, and functionality of a building or structure. Structural framing is a fundamental aspect of building construction, involving the assembly of structural elements to create the skeleton or framework of a building. This framework provides support for floors, walls, roofs, and other loads, ensuring the stability and integrity of the structure.
